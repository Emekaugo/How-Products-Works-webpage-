Getting Started on FundACause

step 1

To Sign Up on FundACause, you will be required to create an account

step 2

You can either register by providing your NIN (National Identity Number) or without the NIN if that is your preference

  • Sign up with NIN Enter your NIN details. Don’t have it handy? no worries, simply dial *346# on your phone to retrieve it.
  • Sign up without NIN Click on the “I don’t have NIN” tab and follow the prompts to complete your registration.

step 3

Setup your account type and start receiving funds

Setting up your Account Type

basic

  • Maximum donation at a time ₦50,000
  • Maximum profile limit is ₦300,000
  • Tier 1 identity requirement

standard

  • Maximum donation at a time ₦200,000
  • Maximum profile limit is ₦500,000
  • Tier 2 identity requirement

premium

  • Maximum donation at a time ₦500,000
  • Unlimited profile limit
  • Tier 3 identity requirement
